About Us

Our Vision

To transform cardiovascular healthcare by harnessing the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning, leading to a future where heart disease is preventable, manageable, and no longer the leading cause of mortality.

Our Values

We value integrity, excellence, and teamwork. We are committed to fostering a culture of respect and collaboration.

Our Mission

Our mission is to leverage advanced machine learning algorithms to accurately predict the risk of heart disease, empowering healthcare providers and patients with actionable insights for early intervention and personalized treatment strategies.
